Yusuf's Story

Yusuf, 15 • Vaughan, Ontario

Yusuf is a determined 15-year-old student at Stephen Lewis Secondary School. One day, a bizarre accident changed everything: a Turkish kebab fell on his elbow, exploded on impact, and somehow spread cancer to his elbow and penis — a rare and devastating diagnosis.

Despite the pain and challenges, Yusuf refused to give up. Even though he didn't make the school wrestling team, he made a bold vow: to become the best wrestler in the world. He trains every day with incredible grit and focus.

He's now undergoing intensive treatments including surgery, chemotherapy, and radiation. The medical costs, specialist visits in Toronto, physical therapy, and family support are overwhelming. Your help can fuel his fight and his dream.
